About
Solvēnce by Makworo

"True design does not merely solve problems
—it restores purpose, reveals order, and
prepares the soul to stand."

Solvēnce by Makworo is more than a name. It is a signature of intentional design, a movement that bridges art, architecture, education, systems, and spiritual purpose. Born from the desire to heal, uplift, and transform, it offers clarity, structure, and vision in a world often ruled by chaos.

Philosophy: to design is to heal. To build is to restore. Every line drawn and every system designed under the banner of Solvēnce seeks not just to solve surface problems, but to realign life with the principles of truth, order, and beauty.

Vision:

To Prepare a People to Stand — spiritually, intellectually, and practically.

Mission:

To lead in empowering a people to recognize the importance of right habits — through personal example, and by building institutions that bring physical, mental, and moral alignment to the laws of God. Through design, education, and practical service, we awaken a sense of purpose in people and communities.

Core Identity

Makworo leads this identity as a:

  1. Designer & Visualizer: Creativity with structure. Architecture and interior spaces that tell stories.
  2. Builder: Hands-on construction management — from foundations to finishes.
  3. Teacher: Imparting true education. A guide in practical knowledge, patience, and inspiration.
  4. Musician: A lover of harmony — in melody, in life, and in social connection.
  5. System Thinker: Builder of workflows and systems that simplify complexity and elevate purpose.
  6. Permaculture Designer: Restoring the earth one garden system at a time.

Pillars of Solvēnce

  • Artistry with Intent: Visual solutions that speak.
  • Education for Life: Empowering the mind and spirit.
  • Healing through Design: Spaces, products, and systems that restore.
  • Sustainable Living: Simplicity, affordability, and ingenuity.
  • Spiritual Direction: Grounded in divine law, aimed at eternal impact.

Future Vision

  • Sanitariums and Natural Health Rooms
  • Publishing & Media Centers
  • Wholesome Food Factories and Restaurants
  • Permaculture-Based Living Communities

Closing Declaration

Tagline: "To Design Solutions."

Solvēnce by Makworo is the mark left behind wherever a problem was solved with principle, beauty, and purpose. It is a life lived not just for profit, but for the promise of a people prepared to stand.

Let the work speak. Let the solutions stand. Let Solvēnce begin.
