Portfolio · Construction & Site Management

Construction &
Site Management

"To build is to restore — every structure raised
is a statement of order against chaos."

From foundations to finishes, Makworo has led and participated in real construction projects — managing timelines, supervising labour, coordinating materials, and ensuring every element aligns with design intent. This is not theoretical; it is hands, concrete, and purpose.

Core Competencies

🏗 Site Supervision

Day-to-day oversight of construction activities — quality checks, labour coordination, and progress reporting.

📐 Setting Out & Surveying

Accurate site setting out from drawings, ensuring structural elements are positioned correctly from the start.

📋 BOQ & Procurement

Bills of Quantities preparation, supplier sourcing, and material cost control to keep projects within budget.

🧱 Masonry & Finishing

Hands-on experience in masonry, plastering, tiling, and other finishing works — bridging management and practice.

📅 Project Scheduling

Programme of works planning, milestone tracking, and client progress reporting for residential and institutional builds.

🌿 Sustainable Building

Applying sustainable and low-cost building techniques — natural materials, passive design, and resource efficiency.
