Design &
Visualization

"Every line drawn is a declaration of intent.
Every space designed is a stage for human purpose."

The design arm of Solvēnce blends architectural precision with artistic vision. Using an integrated toolkit spanning 3D modeling, quantity surveying, and Adobe's creative ecosystem, every project is taken from rough concept to presentation-ready visualization.

Tools of the Trade

SketchUp Pro Quantifier Pro Microsoft Excel Adobe Photoshop Adobe Illustrator Adobe InDesign Adobe Acrobat

The Design Workflow

Every project follows a structured, repeatable process that ensures clarity at every stage:

1
Brief & Concept — Understanding the client's needs, site conditions, and vision. Sketching spatial relationships and functional zones.
2
3D Modeling (SketchUp) — Building accurate 3D models of the space, exploring massing, form, and proportions before committing to detail.
3
Quantification (Quantifier Pro) — Extracting material takeoffs, area calculations, and cost estimates directly from the 3D model.
4
BOQ & Costing (Excel) — Structured Bills of Quantities, budgeting spreadsheets, and financial summaries for client and contractor review.
5
Presentation (Adobe Suite) — Photorealistic renders, branded presentation boards, and print-ready PDFs via Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.
