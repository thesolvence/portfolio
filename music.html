Music &
Harmony

"Music is not entertainment — it is medicine.
Harmony is not a luxury — it is alignment."

Music at Solvēnce is both art and education. Using professional notation and audio production tools, Makworo creates sheet music, arrangements, and training materials that build musicianship — particularly in choral, worship, and ensemble settings. The goal is to make good music accessible, teachable, and deeply meaningful.

Production Tools

Sibelius Adobe Audition Music Notation Choral Arrangement Audio Editing Music Training Materials

What Solvēnce Offers in Music

🎼 Sheet Music & Notation

Original arrangements and compositions created in Sibelius — SATB choral parts, solo pieces, and training exercises in PDF and MIDI formats.

🎙 Audio Production

Clean, professional audio recordings and reference tracks produced in Adobe Audition — ideal for choir rehearsal, personal study, and music training.

📚 Music Education Materials

Structured music training content — sight-reading exercises, ear training, and theory handouts tailored for community and school music programs.

🎹 Arrangements on Request

Custom arrangements of hymns, folk songs, and original compositions for choirs, small ensembles, and educational institutions.
