Permaculture &
Garden Systems

"Design with nature, not against it.
Restore what was broken. Grow what sustains."

Permaculture is design rooted in ecological ethics — care for the earth, care for people, and sharing the surplus. At Solvēnce, permaculture projects are planned as living systems: food forests, garden zones, water harvesting, soil restoration, and community growing spaces that produce abundance while healing the land.

Design Principles in Practice

🌱

Observe & Interact

Reading the land before touching it — sun, water, wind, and life patterns guide every design.

💧

Catch & Store Energy

Swales, rainwater harvesting, and soil carbon retention for resilience through dry seasons.

🌳

Food Forest Layers

7-layer food forest design from canopy to ground cover — maximum yield, minimum input.

♻️

No Waste

Composting, mulching, and closed-loop nutrient cycles that feed the soil that feeds us.

🐝

Biodiversity

Polyculture planting, companion plants, and habitat corridors for beneficial insects and wildlife.

🏡

Community Living

Designing toward self-sustaining permaculture communities — the long vision of Solvēnce.
